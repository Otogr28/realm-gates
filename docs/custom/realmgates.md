!!! abstract "Dimension travel &amp; progression"
    A system that controls **which dimensions you can travel to, and when**. It's the backbone of
    how this server's world and progression are shaped.

## What it does, simply

In normal Minecraft you can wander to the Nether or End whenever you want. Realm Gates changes that:
**travel between dimensions is controlled by rules**. Realms open up as you meet certain conditions
(progress, items, or beating a boss), so the world unfolds in an intended order instead of all at
once.

## What you'll actually notice

- **You spawn in a custom overworld.** On this server your home dimension is a fresh, normal-feeling
  overworld made by Realm Gates — not the default one. It looks and plays like a regular overworld;
  it's just the designated starting realm.
- **Some destinations are gated.** Trying to travel somewhere you haven't unlocked yet shows a
  message explaining you can't go there *yet*. Meet the requirement and the route opens.
- **Reaching the Nether and the End may work differently.** Because of how the home realm is set up,
  natural portals/strongholds can be disabled — you may reach the **Nether** and **End** through
  Realm Gates routes instead. Ask the admin how it's currently configured.
- **Your progress sticks.** Realms you've unlocked and places you've visited are remembered, even
  through death and rejoining.

!!! tip "If you feel 'stuck'"
    Being blocked from a dimension is usually intentional — you haven't met its requirement yet. The
    deny message tells you where you tried to go. Check what you might be missing (an advancement, an
    item, a boss), or ask the admin.

## Why a custom mod for this?

It lets the server present a **curated journey**: a chain of realms that open one after another,
each with its own feel and entry condition. It also powers any **custom realms** unique to this
server. Everything is decided **on the server**, so it's consistent for everyone.
