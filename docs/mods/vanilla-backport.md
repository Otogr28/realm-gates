This pack runs on Minecraft 1.20.1, but newer versions added some nice things. Vanilla Backport brings a selection of those **newer official features back into 1.20.1**, so you don't miss out while staying on the version all the mods need.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it brings back</p>

- Blocks, items and tweaks from later Minecraft updates.
- Everything fits the vanilla look and feel — no clashing styles.
- Lets the pack stay on stable 1.20.1 while still feeling current.
