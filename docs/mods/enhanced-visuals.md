Adds **screen effects** that react to what's happening to you, for a more immersive, cinematic feel. Take fire damage and the edges of your screen flicker with flame; get hurt and you'll see a blood splatter; go underwater or get blinded and the screen responds.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you'll see</p>

- **Fire, blood and impact** overlays when you take damage.
- Water, splash and other environmental effects on screen.
- More drama in combat — handy as a "you're in trouble" cue against tough enemies.
