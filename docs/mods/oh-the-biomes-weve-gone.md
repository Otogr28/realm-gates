This is the mod that makes Aincrad feel huge. It adds **54 brand-new overworld biomes** — from real-world inspired deserts and badlands to fantasy lands like Skyris Vale, Enchanted Tangle and Rainbow Beach — plus **24 new types of wood**, new plants, fruit and foods.

It's mostly about **exploration and building**: more places to discover, more materials to gather, more views worth a screenshot. Combat-wise it's gentle — only three creatures of its own, and only one of them is really dangerous.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you'll find</p>

- **54 new biomes**, sorted by climate — snowy peaks, cold conifer forests, lush wetlands, blossoming groves, scorching deserts and badlands.
- **24 new woods** (baobab, sakura, redwood, jacaranda, maple, willow, cherry-like… each a full building set with planks, stairs, doors and boats).
- New **foods** to forage and cook: baobab fruit, yucca, blueberries (→ pie), white puffballs, aloe vera juice and more.
- Decorative touches like seasonal **wreaths** and a couple of new music discs.
