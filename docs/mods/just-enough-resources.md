An add-on for **[JEI](jei.md)** that answers the "where do I get this?" questions. Look up an ore to see **what heights it spawns at**, look up a mob to see **everything it drops**, or check which biome a plant grows in — all inside the same JEI window.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it tells you</p>

- **Ores** — which world heights they generate at, so you know where to dig.
- **Mobs** — full drop lists and the chances for each.
- **Plants, dungeon loot and villager trades** — at a glance.

!!! tip "Pairs with JEI"
    Just hover an item and open its JEI info — the resource tabs show up right alongside the recipe.
