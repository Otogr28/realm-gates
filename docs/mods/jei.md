**JEI** is the single most useful tool in the pack. It shows every item in the game down the side of your inventory and, with one click, tells you **how to craft it** — or what you can craft *with* it. With this many mods installed, it's how you'll learn the recipes.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">How to use it</p>

- Open your inventory and use the **search box** at the bottom-right to find any item.
- **Hover** an item and press **R** to see its recipe, or **U** to see what it's used in.
- Click an item to jump straight to its recipe; click again to chain through related recipes.

!!! tip "It works with everything"
    JEI reads recipes from every mod in the pack, so it covers Ars Nouveau, Ice and Fire, Supplementaries and the rest automatically.
