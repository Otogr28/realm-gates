The pack's big **magic system**. Instead of pre-made spells, you **build your own** by combining glyphs — a "form" (like Projectile or Touch) followed by "effects" (Break, Ignite, Heal, Launch…). Mix and match to create everything from a mining beam to a healing aura.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Where to start</p>

- Craft the **Worn Notebook** — it's your in-game guide to everything Ars Nouveau.
- Make a **Spell Book**, then **inscribe** glyphs onto it to design custom spells.
- Grow **Source** (the mod's magic energy) and automate it with magical machines and helpful **familiars**.

!!! tip "Read the notebook"
    Ars Nouveau is deep — the Worn Notebook explains each glyph and mechanic step by step. It's the best place to learn.
