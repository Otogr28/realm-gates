A huge collection of **decorative and functional blocks** that feel like they could have been in vanilla. Sconces, signs, lanterns, flower boxes, faucets, urns, cannons, a globe, a hourglass and dozens more — small touches that make builds come alive.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">A taste of what's inside</p>

- **Decoration:** sconces, candle holders, flower boxes, sign posts, pedestals.
- **Functional:** safes for storing valuables, faucets and item pipes, jars and urns.
- **Fun:** a working cannon, a spinning globe, a hourglass, a "speaker block" and more.

!!! tip "Browse it in JEI"
    There are so many blocks that the easiest way to see them all is to search "Supplementaries" in **[JEI](jei.md)**.
