Big, **wearable backpacks** that hugely expand your carrying capacity. They come with built-in extras like a crafting grid and fluid tanks, and there are themed designs based on different mobs to collect.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you get</p>

- A large inventory you **wear on your back** and can access on the go.
- A built-in **2×2 crafting grid** and **fluid tanks** (carry water, lava, etc.).
- Many craftable designs, plus the option to set a personal **sleeping bag** for spawn.

!!! tip "Perfect for long trips"
    With 50+ biomes to explore, a backpack means fewer trips home. Great alongside **[Waystones](waystones.md)**.
