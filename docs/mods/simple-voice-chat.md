Real **proximity voice chat** in Minecraft. Talk to players near you and hear them get louder or quieter with distance — no Discord call needed. It's also the foundation our custom **[Voice Translate](../custom/voice-translate.md)** feature is built on.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you get</p>

- **Proximity voice** — nearby players hear you; far-away ones don't.
- Push-to-talk or voice activation, with an in-game settings menu.
- Groups and (with our setup) live **translation** of what people say.

!!! tip "Set it up first"
    Open the voice chat settings to pick your microphone and key bindings. See the **[Voice &amp; chat translation](../getting-started/voice-and-chat.md)** guide to get going.
