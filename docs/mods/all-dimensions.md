This is the **full list of gameplay mods** in the pack. Click any card to open a short, friendly page
explaining what the mod does — and from there, click its **image** to jump to the official download
&amp; documentation page.

Each card is tagged by where it belongs: <span class="sb-tier-dot sb-tier-dot--all"></span>**All
dimensions** mods are the universal tools (UI, performance, combat, travel, magic, social) that follow you everywhere,
while <span class="sb-tier-dot sb-tier-dot--unassigned"></span>**Unassigned** mods are world/creature/structure content
that hasn't been pinned to a specific realm yet — once a realm opens, they'll be tagged with its name here.

!!! info "Looking for the background libraries?"
    The pack also includes ~26 **libraries / APIs** (Architectury, GeckoLib, KubeJS, Citadel,
    TerraBlender…). They're dependencies other mods need — nothing to use or learn — so they don't
    get their own pages here.
