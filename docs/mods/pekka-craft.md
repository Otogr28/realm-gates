Inspired by Clash Royale, this mod lets you craft and tame two armored knight companions — the fast little **Mini P.E.K.K.A.** and the heavy-hitting **P.E.K.K.A.** They don't spawn in the wild; you build them yourself, then power them up through Hero, Star and Evolution modes. There are also two netherite swords and a Rage potion to round out your kit.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Your two companions</p>

<div class="md-typeset"><table>
<thead><tr><th>Companion</th><th>❤️ Health</th><th>Damage</th><th>How you get it</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><strong>Mini P.E.K.K.A.</strong></td><td>100</td><td>18</td><td>Use the Mini Pekka Spawn Egg. Fast and aggressive.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>P.E.K.K.A.</strong></td><td>200</td><td>30</td><td>Built like an Iron Golem (4 iron blocks) with a P.E.K.K.A. Head on top — tamed to whoever places the head.</td></tr>
</tbody></table></div>

<p class="md-typeset">Both follow you, defend you, and obey sit/follow commands like a tamed pet. Both are immune to knockback.</p>

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Powering them up</p>

<div class="md-typeset"><table>
<thead><tr><th>Mode</th><th>For</th><th>What it does</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><strong>Hero</strong> (Hero Crystal)</td><td>Mini P.E.K.K.A.</td><td>+50% attack damage. With a <strong>Pancake</strong> ready, it heals 40% of its health and gets a 30-second damage boost.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Star</strong> (Star Item)</td><td>Mini P.E.K.K.A.</td><td>Cosmetic star upgrade — needs 50 kills first.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Evolution</strong> (Evolution Crystal)</td><td>P.E.K.K.A.</td><td>Lifesteal on hit (heals 3.5–15% per strike) plus +30% attack speed.</td></tr>
</tbody></table></div>

!!! tip "Keep them fed"
    A **Pancake** (wheat + egg + sugar + milk) heals the Mini P.E.K.K.A. by 2 health — and triggers its special ability when it's in Hero mode.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Weapons &amp; gear</p>

- **PK Sword** — netherite, roughly +10.5 total damage, made via smithing.
- **P.E.K.K.A. Sword** — netherite, +16 damage; upgraded from the PK Sword.
- **Mini Pekka Head** — both a crafting ingredient and a **helmet** (+3 armor).
- **Rage Potion** — a throwable that gives everyone in a ~5-block radius the **Rage** effect for 30 seconds: +35% movement speed and +3 attack damage.
