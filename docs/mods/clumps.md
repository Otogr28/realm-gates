Clumps groups nearby **XP orbs** into a single orb instead of dozens of separate ones. When you kill a big mob or empty a grinder, that's a huge reduction in lag — and you still get exactly the same experience.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Why it helps</p>

- Merges scattered XP orbs so the game tracks far fewer entities.
- Big difference at mob farms and after boss fights.
- You collect the same total XP, just without the stutter.
