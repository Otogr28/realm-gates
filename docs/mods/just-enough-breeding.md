A small but handy **[JEI](jei.md)** add-on: it shows **what each animal eats to breed**. Hover a cow, a horse, or a modded creature and JEI tells you the food you need to make babies — no more guessing.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it does</p>

- Adds a **breeding tab** to any animal's JEI info.
- Covers vanilla animals and many modded creatures.
- Saves you a trip to the wiki when you're setting up a farm.
