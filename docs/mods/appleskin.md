A tiny quality-of-life mod that makes food make sense. Hover any food item and AppleSkin shows **how much hunger and saturation it restores**, and previews how full you'll be before you eat it.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it adds to the HUD</p>

- **Hunger &amp; saturation values** on every food tooltip.
- A preview of the hunger you'll regain, shown right on the food bar.
- Your hidden **saturation** level, so you know when you'll start getting hungry again.
