A set of small, tasteful tweaks (from the makers of Supplementaries) that make **vanilla blocks more interactive and better-looking**. Nothing game-changing — just lots of "oh, nice" moments.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Examples of what it changes</p>

- **Lanterns** hang and sway from chains and walls properly.
- **Candles** can be placed on more surfaces and lit/relit with style.
- **Item display** improvements — mob heads, signs and more become more expressive.
- Various quality-of-life polish on everyday blocks.
