!!! note "On this server, the End works differently"
    Because of how **[Realm Gates](../custom/realmgates.md)** sets up the world, strongholds and the
    natural End portal may be disabled — you reach the **Nether** and **End** through Realm Gates
    instead. Ask the admin for the current setup.

!!! warning "Powerful — and heavy"
    Dragons (a grown one is ~500 HP and can level a base), hydras, cyclopes and the Dread Queen are
    tough fights *and* heavy on performance. Some break blocks. Pick your battles and your
    battlefields.

---

**The strongest-gear path, in one line:** survive [Alex's Caves](mid-game.md#alexs-caves) and the new
biomes for materials → master [Epic Fight](early-game.md) combat → hunt **dragons** for scale armor →
finish at the **End**.
