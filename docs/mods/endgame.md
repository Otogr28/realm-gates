# Endgame — *rank 7–9*

The hardest content and the strongest gear in the pack. These are goals to work toward, not things
you'll tackle on day one.

### YUNG's Better Strongholds — *rank 7*
Reworks strongholds into **large, dangerous dungeons** worth exploring — the traditional gateway
toward the End.

!!! note "On this server"
    Because of how **[Realm Gates](../custom/realmgates.md)** sets up the world, strongholds and the
    natural End portal may be disabled — you reach the **Nether** and **End** through Realm Gates
    instead. Check with the admin / the [Realm Gates page](../custom/realmgates.md) for the current
    setup.

### Ice and Fire: Dragons — *rank 8* {#ice-and-fire-dragons}
The pack's signature endgame: **dragons** (fire, ice, and lightning) plus a roster of mythical
beasts — cyclopes, hydras, gorgons, sirens, sea serpents, giant ant colonies (Myrmex), and an
undead **Dread** faction led by a Lich.

- Dragons **grow with age** over many in-game days; a full-grown one (around 500 HP) can level a
  base. You can **tame one from an egg** and eventually **fly** it.
- Early on, the play is **raiding dens** for eggs and gems rather than fighting adults head-on.
- The reward at the top: **dragon-scale armor and weapons** — the strongest gear in the pack — plus
  dragonbone, dragonsteel, and more.

!!! warning "Powerful and heavy"
    Dragons, hydras, cyclopes and the Dread Queen are tough fights *and* heavy on server
    performance. Some can break blocks. Pick your battles, and your battlefields.

### YUNG's Better End Island — *rank 9*
Redesigns the central **End island** and the area around the **dragon fight** into a proper
climactic destination — the natural finale of a playthrough.

---

*Want the strongest gear path in one line?* Survive **[Alex's Caves](mid-game.md#alexs-caves)** and
the new biomes for materials → gear up with **[Epic Fight](early-game.md)** combat → hunt
**dragons** for scale armor → finish at the **End**.
