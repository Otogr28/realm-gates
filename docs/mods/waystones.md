Waystones are **fast-travel points** you can teleport between. Find them in villages and out in the wild, or craft and place your own. Activate one and you can warp to any other you've discovered — a lifesaver across a big, biome-filled world.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">How it works</p>

- **Right-click** a waystone to activate it and add it to your network.
- Use any activated waystone to **teleport** to another you've unlocked.
- Craft a **Warp Stone** or **Return Scroll** to teleport without standing at one.

!!! tip "Set up a hub"
    Place a waystone at your base and at favourite spots, then hop between them instantly. Pairs perfectly with **[JourneyMap](journeymap.md)** waypoints.
