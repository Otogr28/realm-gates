A privacy mod that removes Minecraft's built-in **chat-reporting** system on this server. Chat stays between us and the people we play with, rather than being cryptographically signed and reportable to Mojang.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it does</p>

- Disables the signed-chat / reporting feature for our server.
- Keeps your messages private to the community here.
- Purely behind-the-scenes — chat works exactly as normal.
