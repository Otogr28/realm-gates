<p class="sb-eyebrow">How to sail</p>

- Build a ship from a **boat (or chest-boat) + sails + leads**, then right-click to board.
- Press **R** to raise/lower the sail; **W/S** change how much sail is out (faster ↔ slower).
- Right-click with a **cannon** to mount it on the deck, keep **cannonballs** in the hold, and fire with **Space**.
- Galley and Drakkar can **row** for extra speed; the Drakkar smashes through sea ice.

!!! note "It's a beta"
    Small Ships is a polished but still-in-development mod, so expect the occasional rough edge while sailing.
