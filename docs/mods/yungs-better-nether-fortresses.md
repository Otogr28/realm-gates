A complete redesign of the Nether fortress: **bigger, scarier and far more interesting** to explore. Sprawling halls, towers and bridges replace the boxy vanilla layout, with new rooms, decoration and loot to find — and plenty of blazes guarding it.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What changes</p>

- Large, atmospheric fortresses that feel like a real stronghold of the Nether.
- New rooms, traps and treasure worth braving the lava for.
- Still your go-to spot for blaze rods and wither skeletons — just more of a journey.

!!! note "Reaching the Nether"
    On this server, Nether access is governed by **[Realm Gates](../custom/realmgates.md)** — sometimes through gates rather than vanilla portals. Ask the admin about the current setup.
