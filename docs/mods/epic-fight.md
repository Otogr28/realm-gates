Epic Fight turns Minecraft's click-to-swing combat into a full **action game**. Weapons get real movesets and combos, you can **dodge** and **parry**, and fights feel like a proper character-action brawler — perfect for taking on this pack's dragons and bosses.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Getting started</p>

- Press **R** to toggle **battle mode** — this switches you into the action combat stance.
- Each weapon type (sword, greatsword, spear, axe…) has its own **combo attacks**.
- Learn to **dodge** and **parry** to survive the tougher enemies.

!!! tip "Try different weapons"
    The feel changes a lot between weapon types — experiment to find your style before facing an Ice and Fire dragon.
