If you like fighting from a distance, this is your mod. It adds **39 craftable bows** and **22 special arrows** — from a stronger Netherite bow to arrows that explode, teleport you, or call down lightning. There are no new mobs or biomes here; it's all about gear you craft and shoot.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">How the damage works</p>

Every shot deals the vanilla bow damage **plus** a fixed bonus from the **bow** **plus** a fixed bonus from the **arrow** — so a strong bow with a strong arrow really stacks up. The bow's bonus only applies when *you* (a player) fire it.

!!! tip "Bow + arrow combos"
    Some effects only trigger with the right bow. A **Copper Bow + Copper Arrow** guarantees lightning; a **Paper/Moss Bow + Paper/Moss Arrow** rolls a random outcome each shot — fire, a teleport swap, lightning, an explosion, or even a few summoned chickens or zombies.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Custom enchantments</p>

There are **7 bow-only enchantments**: **Quick Pull** (draws twice as fast), **Monster Hunter** (extra damage vs hostiles), **Fluid Movement** (move faster while aiming), **Tempo Thief** (slows the enemy, speeds you up), **Anti-Gravity** (levitates the target), and **Bonus Shot / Defensive Shot** (fire extra arrows).
