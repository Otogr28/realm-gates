# The mods

summerBuddies bundles dozens of mods. To keep things readable, this wiki groups the **content and
quality-of-life mods players actually interact with** by a **0–10 progression ranking** — roughly
*when* in your game each one starts to matter.

!!! note "It's a *timing* scale, not a quality score"
    Rank **0** = useful from minute one (maps, performance, social, fast travel). Rank **9** =
    endgame (dragons, the End). A rank-0 mod like JEI is just as essential as any boss mod.

## Custom mods (made for this server)

Three mods were built specifically for summerBuddies and have their own pages:

- **[Realm Gates](../custom/realmgates.md)** — controls dimension travel and progression.
- **[Custom Companions](../custom/custom-companions.md)** — buildable companions that fight with you.
- **[Voice Translate](../custom/voice-translate.md)** — real-time voice & chat translation.

---

!!! info "Where do the libraries go?"
    The pack also includes ~20 **libraries / APIs** (Architectury, GeckoLib, KubeJS, Citadel,
    TerraBlender, and friends). These are background dependencies that other mods need — there's
    nothing to "use" or learn, so this wiki doesn't give them individual pages.
