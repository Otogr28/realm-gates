Deep below Aincrad are **six exotic cave worlds**, each one its own themed mini-realm with unique creatures, blocks and loot. They don't appear on the surface — you have to **hunt them down with a map** you craft from a Cave Tablet, which makes finding each one a little adventure of its own.

## Notable creatures

There are **40+ creatures** across the caves, from 2 HP minnows to 600 HP titans. Here are the headliners — you'll discover the rest as you explore.

!!! warning "Terrain damage"
    The **Nucleeper** and the craftable **Nuclear Bomb** cause chunk-eating explosions that can wreck builds. Keep them well away from shared areas and other people's bases.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Why go down there</p>

Each cave drops materials you can't get anywhere else — uranium for bombs and reactors, magnetic neodymium for gadgets, pure darkness for late-game gear, dino meat and bone for armor, and more.
