A live **minimap** in the corner of your screen plus a **full-screen map** you can open any time. It fills in as you explore, so you'll never be completely lost — essential when you're roaming across 50+ new biomes.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you get</p>

- A **minimap** showing terrain, nearby mobs and other players.
- A **full-screen map** (default key **J**) you can pan and zoom.
- **Waypoints** — mark a spot, give it a name, and get an on-screen pointer back to it.

!!! tip "Mark your base"
    Drop a waypoint on your home and any cool biomes you find — then use **[Waystones](waystones.md)** to fast-travel back.
