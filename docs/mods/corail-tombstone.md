Death doesn't have to mean losing everything. When you die, your items are kept safe in a **grave** at the spot — so you can run back and recover them without panic. There's also an optional layer of **soul magic** for those who want to dig deeper.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it gives you</p>

- A **grave** holds your full inventory where you died — no more items burning in lava or despawning.
- A **Grave Key** and on-screen marker help you find and reclaim it.
- Optional **soul-bound enchantments, knowledge and perks** for players who want extra depth.

!!! tip "Don't rush back recklessly"
    Your stuff is safe in the grave, so take a breath, re-gear, and recover it carefully — especially if a dragon is involved.
