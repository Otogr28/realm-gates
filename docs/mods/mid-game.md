# Mid game — *rank 4–6*

Once you're established — iron and early diamond, a base, some fast travel — these mods open up
exploration, logistics, and the first real "dungeon" worlds.

## Logistics & travel

### Easy Villagers — *rank 4*
Makes **villager trading and management** far simpler: pick villagers up, see and reroll their
trades easily, and build compact trading setups without the usual hassle.

### Traveler's Backpack — *rank 4*
Big **wearable backpacks** with extra abilities — built-in crafting, fluid tanks, and special
perks depending on the backpack. A huge inventory and utility upgrade for long trips.

### Small Ships — *rank 4*
Adds proper **navigable boats and ships** you can sail, with cargo and cannons on some. Turns the
ocean and rivers into real travel routes — pairs well with the new coastal biomes.

## Underground & structures

### Alex's Caves — *rank 6* {#alexs-caves}
Six **exotic cave worlds** hidden under the Overworld, each a self-contained ecosystem with its own
creatures, blocks, food, and weapons. You can dip in fairly early but mastering them is a late-game
goal.

| Cave | Theme | What you'll meet |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Magnetic Caves** | metal / magnetism *(recommended first)* | Flying robots, magnetic blocks that push/pull your iron gear. |
| **Primordial Caves** | prehistoric jungle | Dinosaurs — some **rideable/tameable** (Tremorsaurus, Subterranodon). |
| **Toxic Caves** | nuclear wasteland | Radiation hazards and the **Nucleeper**, a creeper whose blast eats chunks. |
| **Abyssal Chasm** | deep ocean | A rideable submarine and the **Deep Ones** faction with reputation. |
| **Forlorn Hollows** | gothic / vampiric | The most dangerous, late-game area. |
| **Candy Cavity** | surreal sweets | Whimsical candy creatures (and a worm boss). |

!!! warning "Heads up: terrain damage"
    The **Nucleeper** (and the craftable Nuclear Bomb) cause **chunk-eating explosions** that can
    flatten builds. Be careful bringing them near your base or shared areas.

### YUNG's Better Nether Fortresses — *rank 6*
Redesigns Nether fortresses into **bigger, more interesting structures** to explore and fight
through — a real step up from vanilla's repetitive corridors.
