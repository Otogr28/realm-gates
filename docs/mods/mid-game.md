### The six caves of Alex's Caves {#alexs-caves}

| Cave | Theme | What you'll meet |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Magnetic Caves** | metal / magnetism *(start here)* | Flying robots; magnetic blocks that push/pull your iron gear. |
| **Primordial Caves** | prehistoric jungle | Dinosaurs — some rideable/tameable. |
| **Toxic Caves** | nuclear wasteland | Radiation and the **Nucleeper**, a creeper whose blast eats chunks. |
| **Abyssal Chasm** | deep ocean | A rideable submarine and the **Deep Ones** faction. |
| **Forlorn Hollows** | gothic / vampiric | The most dangerous, late-game area. |
| **Candy Cavity** | surreal sweets | Whimsical candy creatures (and a worm boss). |

!!! warning "Heads up: terrain damage"
    The **Nucleeper** (and the craftable Nuclear Bomb) cause **chunk-eating explosions** that can
    flatten builds. Keep that away from shared areas and other people's builds.
