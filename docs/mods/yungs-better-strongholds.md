Vanilla strongholds are a maze of identical stone rooms. **YUNG's Better Strongholds** replaces them with large, hand-crafted dungeons full of new rooms, decorations, secret passages, traps and proper loot — the kind of place that's actually worth exploring on the way to the End portal.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What changes</p>

- Big, varied layouts with libraries, prisons, crypts and hidden vaults.
- New decorative blocks and details that make each stronghold feel built, not generated.
- Better treasure — but watch for traps and tougher encounters.

!!! note "Reaching the End"
    On this server, how you get to the Nether and End is handled by **[Realm Gates](../custom/realmgates.md)** — sometimes through gates rather than vanilla portals. Ask the admin about the current setup.
