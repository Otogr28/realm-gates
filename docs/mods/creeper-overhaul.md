The plain green creeper is gone. In its place are **16 biome-specific creepers**, each with its own look, behavior and themed drops. The creeper you run into now tells you something about where you are — a sandy one in the desert, a mossy one in caves, an explosive pufferfish in warm oceans.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Three kinds of creeper</p>

- **Explosive** — the classic boom (a bit bigger if charged by lightning).
- **Melee** — these <em>don't</em> explode; they fight you hand-to-hand instead (bamboo, savannah, snowy, ocean).
- **Passive** — the mushroom creeper never attacks and only runs away.

Some are **neutral** (bamboo, hills, savannah, snowy) and leave you alone unless you hit them first.
