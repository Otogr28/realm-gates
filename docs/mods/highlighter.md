A subtle visual helper: Highlighter draws a clean outline around the **exact block you're pointing at**. It's especially handy when you're placing blocks precisely or working with tricky modded shapes.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it does</p>

- Outlines the block under your crosshair so there's no doubt which one you'll hit.
- Replaces vanilla's faint selection box with a crisper, easier-to-see edge.
- Lightweight and purely visual — no gameplay changes.
