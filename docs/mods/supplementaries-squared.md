An add-on to **Supplementaries** that fills in the gaps with extra decorative and functional blocks — colored sacks, golden candle holders, metal frames, plaques, daub stairs and slabs, and one neat utility item. Everything here is crafted; there are no new mobs or biomes.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you can build</p>

<div class="md-typeset"><table>
<thead><tr><th>Addition</th><th>What it is</th></tr></thead>

<tr><td><strong>Colored Sacks</strong> (16 colors)</td><td>Dye the portable storage sack from Supplementaries any color — it keeps its contents.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Gold Candle Holders</strong> (21)</td><td>A golden holder for any candle, in every color (candle + gold ingot).</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Plaques</strong> (iron / gold / copper)</td><td>Metal wall signs for decoration — three of each from a row of ingots.</td></tr>

!!! tip "The Heavy Key"
    The one bit of real gameplay here is the **Heavy Key** (Supplementaries key + netherite scrap). Unlike a normal key — whose "password" is just its name and can be copied by renaming — the Heavy Key uses the *item itself* as the password, so every one you craft is **unique and impossible to duplicate**. A perfect master key.
