FancyMenu is what powers the custom **Realm Gates main-menu theme** you see when you launch the game. It lets menus be redesigned with custom backgrounds, buttons and animations, giving the pack its own identity from the very first screen.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it's for</p>

- Drives the **themed title screen** and menus for this modpack.
- Custom backgrounds, panels and animated touches.
- You don't need to configure anything — it just makes the menus look the part.
