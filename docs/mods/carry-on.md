Carry On lets you **pick up and carry** chests, furnaces, machines — even mobs — and set them down somewhere else, contents and all. No more emptying a chest block-by-block just to move your base.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What you can do</p>

- **Pick up a container** (chest, barrel, furnace…) <em>with its contents</em> and carry it.
- Scoop up **animals and small mobs** to relocate them.
- Place everything back down exactly as it was.

!!! note "Sneak to grab"
    Look at the block or mob and sneak to pick it up. You'll move slower while carrying something heavy.
