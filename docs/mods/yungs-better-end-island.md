The vanilla central End island is a flat disc with a dragon on it. **YUNG's Better End Island** rebuilds it into a dramatic, layered landscape and gives the Ender Dragon fight a more memorable arena to take place in.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What changes</p>

- A redesigned central island with new terrain, structures and secrets.
- A more cinematic backdrop for the dragon fight.
- Extra exploration and loot once the dragon is down.

!!! note "Reaching the End"
    On this server, End access is governed by **[Realm Gates](../custom/realmgates.md)** — sometimes through gates rather than vanilla portals. Ask the admin about the current setup.
