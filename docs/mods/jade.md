Jade shows a small tooltip at the top of your screen telling you **exactly what you're looking at**. Point at any block or creature and you'll see its name, the mod it's from, and useful extras like the right tool to mine it or a mob's health bar.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it shows</p>

- The **name** of any block or mob, and **which mod** added it.
- The **correct tool** (and tier) needed to harvest a block.
- Live **health** of the creature you're aiming at, plus growth stages, machine progress and more.

!!! tip "Great for a modded pack"
    With so many mods, Jade is the fastest way to figure out "what is this thing and how do I deal with it?"
