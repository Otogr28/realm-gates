A pure performance mod. It stops the game from rendering mobs, animals and block-entities that are **hidden behind walls** — things you can't actually see. The result is more FPS, especially in busy areas, farms and big bases, with no visual difference to you.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Why it helps</p>

- Skips drawing creatures and tile-entities that are out of sight.
- Big wins in mob farms, villages and detailed builds.
- Completely behind-the-scenes — install it and forget it.
