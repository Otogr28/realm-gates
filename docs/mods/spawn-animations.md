A small visual upgrade: instead of mobs **popping into existence**, they rise up out of the ground with a little animation when they spawn. It's a subtle touch that makes the world feel more alive.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">What it changes</p>

- Creatures **emerge from the ground** when they spawn naturally.
- Purely cosmetic — no effect on gameplay or difficulty.
- Works with vanilla and modded mobs alike.
