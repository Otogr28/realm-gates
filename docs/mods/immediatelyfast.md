Another invisible speed-up. ImmediatelyFast optimizes how the game draws **text, items and UI**, which smooths out the frame drops you'd otherwise get with busy inventories, JEI open, or lots of on-screen text.

<p class="sb-eyebrow">Why it helps</p>

- Faster rendering of HUD, tooltips, item icons and menus.
- Noticeable in inventory-heavy moments (browsing JEI, big chests).
- Zero configuration — it just makes things smoother.
